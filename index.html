Who we are?

The University of Ottawa Gentlemen's Room Barber Shop is a one-of-a-kind grooming experience for the modern man. Located in the heart of the university campus, we offer a range of services to help you look and feel your best. From haircuts and shaves to grooming products and accessories, we have everything you need to elevate your style. Our barbers are highly skilled and have a passion for men's grooming. We use only the finest products and tools to ensure a top-notch experience for every client. Whether you're looking for a classic, sophisticated look or something more modern and edgy, we can create a custom style that fits your personality and lifestyle. Step into our luxurious, vintage-inspired barber shop and experience the best in men's grooming. Treat yourself to a day of pampering and leave feeling refreshed, confident, and ready to take on the world.
